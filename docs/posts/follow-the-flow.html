Follow
the Flow

AI & Fluid Simulation · Digital Art

"Follow the Flow" is an immersive art installation combining AI and fluid simulation. Particle movements mimic natural and sound dynamics, while AI-generated colors create a constantly evolving visual flow. This fusion of data, fluid dynamics, and AI explores the balance between order and chaos, nature and technology.
